The Franklin Butchery is a family-owned neighborhood butcher shop in the heart of Franklin, Tennessee — where every cut is hand-selected, every meal is made with intention, and every visit feels like home.

Rooted in Care

Our beef and pork come from Bear Creek Farm, raised with humane handling and pasture-first practices you can trust.

Fresh, Never Frozen

We source fresh-forward seafood that rotates with the seasons. What you see on the ice is what just came in.

Family Owned

Built by a family who believes in doing things the right way — with care, transparency, and a deep love for this community.

Crafted by Hand

Every cut is trimmed and prepared by our in-house butchers in our glass cut room — no shortcuts, no compromises.
