Kazu Burger

Packs a punch of bold, rebellious flavor — almost as much as its namesake! Double patty of Bear Creek dry-aged, Black Angus beef, American cheese, griddled onions, lettuce, tomato, pickles, Butcher Sauce.

Gigi Beef

Appears calm and sweet, but quickly reveals a pleasant spice! House braised, dry-aged Bear Creek Black Angus short ribs, provolone, spicy giardiniera, aioli.

Zygulicious BBQ Pork

Named for the kid who screams “I want pork butt!” at every cookout. Loaded with Bear Creek Heritage pulled pork, Butchery BBQ sauce, house slaw, red onion pickles, cracklings.

Cami Sami Club

Our wild and feisty club. House smoked and slow-roasted turkey breast, pulled Bear Creek Heritage pork, aged cheddar, oven-dried tomatoes, arugula, crispy onions.

Meatball Livvy

A loud, sassy, energetic take on a classic. Dry-aged Bear Creek Black Angus beef meatballs, tomato sauce, provolone cheese, crispy onions, whipped ricotta, hot honey drizzle.

Chicken Bella

Elegant, bright and playful. We hand-bread and golden fry Humane-certified, all-natural chicken and add creamy marinara with a hint of spice, provolone, arugula, robust pesto sauce.
