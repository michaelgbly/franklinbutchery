Bear Creek Farm

Meat the Way
It’s Meant to Taste.

Our beef and pork come from Bear Creek Farm right here in Tennessee. Pasture-raised, grain-finished, with no added hormones or antibiotics. The animals are raised on natural spring water and a proprietary feed blend developed with an animal nutritionist and large-animal veterinarian. Every cut is traceable back to the farm it came from.

No water injection. No fillers. No shortcuts. Just real meat, raised the right way, and you can taste the difference from the very first bite.
