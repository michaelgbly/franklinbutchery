# Lori

**Role:** Co-owner, The Franklin Butchery
**Family:** Married to Greg; their daughter met Carlos & Aubrey's daughter at a school carnival

## Key Quotes & Perspectives
- "More women than you think actually go in and are buying the meats and the seafoods" — advocates for an experience that appeals to women, not just the stereotypical male butcher-shop customer
- Wants an "elevated experience" that's different from any other butcher shop
- Became friends with Aubrey (Carlos's wife) first — the friendship preceded the business
- Ran into Carlos multiple times at Whole Foods unable to get the cuts they wanted — catalyst for the concept
- Practical about the hangout factor — doesn't want the shop to become a co-working space ("she doesn't want people hanging out too much")

## Notes
- Very involved in the business direction and brand positioning
- Strong voice on the demographic insight and experience design
